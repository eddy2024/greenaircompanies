      Welcome to NIIT, a high-tech information technology training institution. We provide various "hands-on" training for in-demand careers in the Information Technology worldwide. NIIT has always been pioneering new industry-relevant programs based on the changing market needs.

      NIIT's curriculum is designed and developed as a result of extensive research done at software factories, universities of higher learning and organizations employing IT resources across the world.       • Alliance


      NIIT has forged an alliance to offer UEW’s acclaimed Degree Programme - B.Sc. Information Technology in Ghana.  With this alliance / partnership, students have acquired a chance to obtain an accredited degree in IT without leaving the country. This degree programme builds upon the study that students would have completed with NIIT.

      This Affiliation with UEW would go a long way in further improving IT expertise in the country. The students will gain dual qualification namely Professional Diploma in IT from NIIT-India along with B Sc Degree in IT from UEW-Ghana.  Under this affiliation, NIIT students will also have the privilege to have advance standing admission in Level 300 directly after completing two years of NIIT program.

      This alliance further builds on the academic validation of NIIT Programmes with nearly 20 universities worldwide and brings UEW’s accredited qualifications to NIIT students, in their own country.
